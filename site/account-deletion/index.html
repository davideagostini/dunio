Account Deletion

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Dunio account and associated data. Dunio is the Android app published by Davide Simone Agostini.

If you want your account deleted, email dsagostini@gmail.com from the Google account used with Dunio and include the subject line Dunio account deletion request.

Operator

  • App name: Dunio
  • Legal name: Davide Simone Agostini
  • Contact email: dsagostini@gmail.com
  • Website: davideagostini.com

How to request deletion

  1. Email dsagostini@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line Dunio account deletion request.
  3. Send the request from the Google account connected to your Dunio profile.
  4. Include any details that help us identify your account, such as your account email.

We may contact you to verify the request before deletion is completed.

What is deleted

When we process a deletion request, we remove the personal data that Dunio controls for your account, including:

  • your user profile data
  • your account record in Firebase Authentication
  • your household membership record
  • invite records directly associated with your account
  • your access to household finance data through Dunio

What may be retained

Dunio is a shared-household app, so some household data may remain available to other household members if it is still needed for the household to function. That can include shared finance records that belong to the household rather than to one person.

We may also retain limited records when needed for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or service integrity.

Backup systems and logs may keep limited copies for a short period before they are overwritten or purged.

Retention period

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days after verification. Some backup or log data may remain for up to 30 additional days before automatic removal.

Shared household data

If your account is part of a household with other members, deleting your own account does not automatically delete the household for everyone else. Shared household data is handled carefully so other members do not lose access unexpectedly.

If the request affects data owned by the entire household, we may ask for clarification before proceeding.

Contact
