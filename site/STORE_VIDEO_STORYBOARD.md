# Dunio Store Video Storyboard

## Goal

Create a short app video for Google Play, the website, and X that quickly explains what Dunio does and why it is useful.

Recommended duration:

- `20-30 seconds`

Recommended style:

- fast
- calm
- no voice-over required
- large text overlays
- real app footage only

## Core Message

Dunio helps couples and households track shared finances in one calm Android app.

The video should communicate:

- shared household finance
- net worth visibility
- expense tracking
- assets and liabilities
- fast entry
- quick access
