FAQ

Questions people usually have before trying Dunio.

A few quick answers about pricing, platform, open-source availability, and shared household access.

Is Dunio free to use?

Yes. Dunio is free to use.

Is Dunio open source?

Yes. Dunio is open source, and the Android app source code is available on GitHub.

Is Dunio available on Google Play?

Yes. Dunio is available in beta on Google Play.

How does shared household access work?

Each person signs in with Google, then creates a household or joins an existing one. People in the same household share the same finance data.

Can I support the project?

You can support ongoing development through Buy Me a Coffee or GitHub Sponsors.
