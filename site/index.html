Features

Everything needed for a shared finance app, nothing heavier.

Summ keeps the product simple: one shared household, one dashboard, quick entry, and a UI designed for everyday mobile use.

Shared household

One household for both members, with shared assets, transactions, and dashboard data.

Net worth overview

Track assets, liabilities, cash flow, savings rate, and runway in one place.

Month-based tracking

Move across months for dashboard, transactions, and assets without leaving the app flow.

Quick entry

Add transactions quickly from the app, from the Quick Settings Tile, or from widgets.

Recurring payments

Track repeat transactions and reduce manual work for monthly expenses and income.

Dark mode and widgets

Use Summ comfortably day and night, with home-screen widgets for faster access.
